Meet the Glitchies

Welcome to the Glitchies! As a community-focused project with a mental health intitiative, our greatest priority is launching our Twitter, website and Discord community. We are aiming to provide our community members with the tools to truly communicate each other in meaningful ways.

Excite-mint!

It’s mint day! Tentatively, our mint price and mint date are in the works and will be announced soon over Discord and Twitter. Glitch List members will receive earlier access to mint. The remaining supply will be available for public sale afterwards. Our mint will be done by Metaseed Labs to ensure that members are able to mint without having to worry about high gas fees or other interruptions!

Building the New World

Glitchies holders will unlock a wide range of hands-on utility to reward their loyalty as supporters of the project. Partnering with the hottest new projects in the NFT space, Glitchies will offer exclusive whitelist giveaways to share opportunities to mint with holders. We will also be hosting giveaways for IRL prizes such as all-inclusive trips, experiences and therapy scholarships

Merchandise

We will be partnering with top manufacturers to bring a nice selection of street-wear inspired clothing pieces. We have a team of designers who will only use tasteful, quality and hand-cut materials for the drop. Holders will be rewarded with a free piece of merchandise.

Glitchie’s Mental Health Center

We will be partnering with a reputable web2 mental health service in order to bring their resources and services to our community members. We will also be creating a virtual space for the Glitchies to interact and create meaningful connections in a supportive and safe environment. We will be offering mental health services for free to our community members from our virtual space and website dashboard.
